Up Front Pricing

$2000 will get you a purely informational, static website which can include:

  • Texts, images, icons, and video to describe your project or business.
  • Links to external content hosted elsewhere.
  • A contact form for visitors to send you emails directly from your website.

Additional functionality will be priced separately. Please contact us for specifics.

Continued Support Pricing

If you just want to be handed a website that you'll host and maintain, then this pricing section does not apply. However, if you want us to provide your domain name, hosting solution, and continued support, then there will be a monthly charge.

$200/month will get you the following:

  • A domain name at no additional charge.
  • Hosting of your website.
  • 2 hours of tech support each month.

Additional services and extra tech support can be added. Please contact us for specifics.
